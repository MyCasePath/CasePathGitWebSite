CasePath User Privacy Policy

At CasePath, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our customers' data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and safeguard your information when using our Software as a Service (SaaS) solution.

1. No Data Sharing Without Consent CasePath does not share, sell, or disclose any customer data to third-party providers or any other entities without the express written consent of the customer. Your data will remain private and secure unless you provide explicit authorization for us to share it.

2. Protection of Patient Data If any patient data is utilized for analytics or internal improvement purposes, CasePath ensures that all personally identifiable information (PII) is fully sanitized and removed prior to use. This means that any data we analyze or process will be anonymized, ensuring that individual patients cannot be identified.

3. Commitment to Data Security We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data and ensure that it is handled responsibly and ethically. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we take every step necessary to maintain the confidentiality and security of the information you entrust to us.

4. Your Control You retain full control over your data, and CasePath will not use or share any of your information without your direct permission. If you have any questions or need further clarification, please contact our support team.

By using our services, you agree to this privacy policy. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection for our customers.
